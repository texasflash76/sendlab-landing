How the challenge works

Simple format, fast setup, transparent scoring.

1. Climbers opt in

Participants can join the challenge and attempt one fixed climb.

2. Score is completion + time

If a climber completes the challenge, their score is the time to completion. If they do not complete it, they are not ranked above finishers.

3. Top performers receive payout

The prize pool is distributed among the top 30 percent of completed performances.

FAQ

Is this free for the gym?

Yes. This one-day pilot costs the gym nothing.

Does gym staff need to run it?

No. Sendlab runs the pilot and operations during the 2-hour window.

How is scoring decided?

Rankings are based on completion first, then the time it took to complete the fixed climb.

How are payouts handled?

The prize pool is distributed to the top 30 percent of performers.

Is there an entry fee?

Yes. Climbers pay a $1 entry fee to participate in the challenge.

What if someone cheats?

Any score we determine to be dishonest is disqualified and not acknowledged.
